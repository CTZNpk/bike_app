##BikeMate

BikeMate is more than just a mileage tracker or maintenance log; it's your personal companion for managing and optimizing your motorcycle's performance. This mobile app simplifies the process of recording petrol refills and maintenance activities, offering insights into your bike's efficiency and health.
Features:
Petrol Fill-ups:

Easily record every petrol refill, including the amount, cost, and odometer reading. Keep track of your fuel expenses and analyze consumption patterns over time.
Maintenance Log:

Never miss a service or maintenance task. Document every repair, oil change, or tune-up to ensure your bike stays in top condition. Set reminders for future maintenance based on your bike's specific requirements.
Mileage Tracking:

Effortlessly monitor your bike's mileage and performance. Get comprehensive statistics on fuel efficiency, average distances, and trends to optimize your rides.
Performance Analytics:

Visualize your bike's performance through intuitive graphs and insights. Identify patterns, understand your riding habits, and make informed decisions to enhance your bike's overall performance.
Why BikeMate?

    Efficiency Boost: Optimize your bike's performance and fuel efficiency by having detailed insights into its usage and maintenance history.

    Easy and Intuitive: A user-friendly interface ensures hassle-free data entry and quick access to vital information.

    Personalized Insights: Tailored statistics and analytics empower you to make informed decisions regarding your bike's upkeep and usage.

BikeMate is designed for motorcycle enthusiasts and daily commuters alike, providing a simple yet powerful tool to manage your bike's health and performance.
